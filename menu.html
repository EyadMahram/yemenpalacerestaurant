Rice Dishes

The foundation of Yemeni cuisine — fragrant long-grain basmati rice layered with slow-cooked meats and aromatic spices.

🥖Signature
Lamb Mandi المندي
€18

Whole lamb slow-roasted in an underground clay oven (tandoor) for 4+ hours over fragrant basmati rice, perfumed with saffron, cardamom, dried limes, and our secret hawaij spice blend. Served with salad and sauce.

🌍 Origin: Hadhramaut, Yemen · Also available with chicken €14

Spice
🍛Favourite
Kabsa الكبسة
€17

Tender chicken or lamb simmered on a bed of long-grain rice with tomatoes, dried apricots, raisins, and warm spices including cinnamon, cloves, and black cardamom. Finished with toasted almonds and crispy fried onions.

🌍 Origin: Throughout Yemen & Arabia · Chicken or Lamb

Spice
🍖Chef's Pick
Zurbian الزربيان
€19

A festive layered rice dish traditionally served at celebrations. Lamb and rice cooked together in a sealed pot with saffron, rose water, and a rich blend of whole spices. The sealed cooking traps all aromatic steam inside.

🌍 Origin: Aden & Hadhramaut, Yemen

Spice
🥑Whole Lamb
Haneeth الحانيذ
€45 serves 2-4

An entire lamb marinated in hawaij spices overnight and slow-roasted whole for up to 8 hours until the meat falls off the bone with the lightest touch. A feast worthy of celebration. Pre-order required (24h notice).

🌍 Origin: Sana'a highlands, Yemen · Pre-order required

Stews & Soups

Deeply flavoured, slow-simmered Yemeni stews served in traditional clay pots with freshly baked bread.

🍲National Dish
Saltah السلتة
€15

Yemen's beloved national dish. A rich lamb and vegetable stew spiced with hawaij and fenugreek, served bubbling in a clay pot and crowned with hulba — a cloud of whipped fenugreek foam that is uniquely Yemeni. Comes with fresh lahoh bread.

🌍 Origin: Sana'a, Yemen · Served in traditional clay pot

Spice
🥣Slow-Cooked
Fahsa الفحسة
€16

Melt-tender lamb pieces slow-braised for hours in a deeply spiced broth until the meat almost dissolves into the sauce. Finished with a generous pour of ghee and fragrant hawaij. A truly warming and restorative dish.

🌍 Origin: Dhamar, central Yemen

Spice
🍰
Aseed العصيد
€8

A traditional Yemeni porridge made from wheat flour cooked into a smooth, thick dough-like consistency. Served warm in a bowl with a generous drizzle of honey, clarified butter, and a sprinkle of spices. Comfort food at its purest.

🌍 Origin: Throughout Yemen · Traditional breakfast & dessert

Spice
🍳
Maraq المرق
€9

A nourishing Yemeni bone broth soup slowly simmered for hours to extract every drop of flavour. Seasoned with turmeric, coriander, and cinnamon. Served with crusty bread for dipping. The Yemeni equivalent of grandmother's cure-all soup.

🌍 Origin: Throughout Yemen · Light & restorative

Spice
🍴Sharing
Fattah الفتة
€13

Layers of toasted flatbread soaked in a rich meat broth, topped with slow-cooked tender lamb or chicken, and finished with a herby tomato sauce and crispy fried onions. A dish built for sharing — best eaten communally from one large plate.

🌍 Origin: Yemeni & Levantine tradition · Great for sharing

Breads & Sides

Freshly made every day — our breads are the soul of the Yemeni table, perfect for scooping and sharing.

🥃Traditional
Lahoh اللحوح
€3

A uniquely Yemeni spongy sourdough flatbread made from a fermented batter of sorghum and wheat. Cooked on one side only to create a honeycomb surface that is perfect for soaking up stews and sauces. The Yemeni answer to injera.

🌍 Traditional Yemeni flatbread · Naturally fermented

🥐
Malawah الملاوح
€4

Flaky, layered flatbread made from a buttery dough folded repeatedly to create thin, crispy, golden layers. Served warm and best enjoyed with honey and a soft-boiled egg for a traditional Yemeni breakfast experience, or alongside a hearty stew.

🌍 South Yemen speciality · Crispy & flaky

🥡Sweet
Bint Al-Sahn بنت الصحن
€6

Yemen's most beloved sweet bread — layers of thin pastry soaked in clarified butter (ghee) and drizzled with black seed honey. Served warm, it melts in your mouth. Traditionally offered to welcome honoured guests and at celebrations.

🌍 Hadhramaut, Yemen · Served with Sidr honey

🍳
Mutabbaq المطبق
€7

A stuffed Yemeni flatbread filled with seasoned minced lamb, egg, spring onion, and spices, folded and pan-fried in ghee to a golden crisp. Also available with egg and cheese (vegetarian). Served with a fresh herb dipping sauce.

🌍 Popular street food · Meat or vegetarian filling

🥑
Samboosa سمبوسة
€5 (3 pcs)

Crispy Yemeni-style fried pastry triangles filled with spiced minced lamb, onion, and fresh coriander. Deep-fried to a perfect golden crunch and served with a tangy tamarind dipping sauce. Perfect as a starter or alongside your main dish.

🌍 Traditional Yemeni appetiser · 3 pieces per serve

Drinks

From our famous spiced Qishr to fresh tropical juices — every drink tells a part of the Yemeni story.

☕Must Try
Qishr القشر
€3.50

Yemen's unique coffee drink brewed from roasted coffee husks (not the bean), spiced with fresh ginger and green cardamom. Pale amber in colour with a smooth, warming character that is entirely unlike espresso. Served in traditional small cups from a dallah pot.

🌍 Yemen's most beloved drink · Caffeine-free option available

🍵Spiced Tea
Shahi الشاهي
€3

Traditional Yemeni spiced milk tea, brewed with black tea, whole spices (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, ginger), and sweetened condensed milk. Rich, warming, and deeply comforting — a staple of every Yemeni household morning and evening.

🌍 Served sweet · Can be made without milk

🍎
Fresh Mango Juice عصير مانجو
€4

A thick, freshly blended mango juice made from ripe tropical mangoes with a touch of lime and a whisper of cardamom. Vibrant, sweet, and utterly refreshing. A beloved drink across the Arabian peninsula and East Africa.

🌍 Made fresh to order · No added sugar

🍊
Tamarind Drink تمر هندي
€3.50

A tangy-sweet Yemeni drink made from dried tamarind, dissolved in water and sweetened with dates or sugar, then chilled and spiced with a pinch of cumin. Deeply refreshing and a great palate cleanser between the rich flavours of a Yemeni feast.

🌍 Traditional Yemeni drink · Served chilled

🥑
Avocado Shake كريمة افوكادو
€5

A luxuriously creamy avocado milkshake blended with chilled milk, a touch of honey, and cardamom. Thick and nourishing, this drink is a popular staple of Yemeni cafes and is particularly beloved in Aden. A meal in a glass.

🌍 Aden speciality · Dairy-free version available

🎅
Dates & Arabic Coffeeتمر و قهوة
€4

The ultimate Yemeni welcome: a small cup of lightly spiced Arabic coffee (not Qishr — this is cardamom-infused green coffee) served alongside a plate of Medjool dates. Offered to every guest as a sign of hospitality.

🌍 The traditional Yemeni welcome · Complimentary refills
