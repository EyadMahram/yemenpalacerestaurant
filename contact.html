Getting Here

Wagenstraat 126A, Den Haag

🚌
By Public Transport

Tram 1, 6, 16 to “Wagenstraat” stop. 2-minute walk. Train to Den Haag Centraal (10-minute walk or tram).

🚘
By Car

Paid parking available on Wagenstraat and nearby streets. Underground parking at “P+R Centrum” (5-min walk).

🚲
By Bike

Bike parking available directly in front of the restaurant on Wagenstraat. The city centre is very cycle-friendly.
